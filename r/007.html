How it started

From a small stand to a nationwide network

FreshMarket was born in a small farmer's market stall in Portland, Oregon. Our founders — two brothers from a farming family — saw firsthand how much fresh produce went to waste because there was no efficient way to connect farms directly with consumers.

In 2018, they launched FreshMarket with 5 partner farms and a single delivery van. The idea was radical: eliminate the middlemen, pay farmers fairly, and deliver produce within 24 hours of harvest.

Today, we partner with over 200 farms across the Pacific Northwest, California, and the Midwest — serving thousands of families who trust us for their weekly groceries.

Values

What we stand for

🌍

Sustainability First

We use 100% compostable packaging, electric delivery vehicles in urban areas, and partner exclusively with farms practicing regenerative agriculture.

🤝

Fair Trade for Farmers

We pay our partner farms an average of 35% above market rates. Every farmer sets their own prices, and we never negotiate down.

🔬

Transparent Sourcing

Every product in our catalog includes detailed origin information — you know exactly which farm grew your food and when it was harvested.

Frequently Asked Questions

We currently serve 12 metropolitan areas across the West Coast, including Portland, Seattle, San Francisco, Los Angeles, San Diego, and their surrounding suburbs. Enter your ZIP code at checkout to check availability.

Orders placed before 2 PM are eligible for same-day delivery. Our fleet of temperature-controlled vehicles ensures your groceries arrive fresh — typically within 2–4 hours of ordering.

If anything arrives damaged or doesn't meet your expectations, contact us within 24 hours for a full refund or replacement — no questions asked. We stand behind every item we deliver.

We offer both organic and conventional options. All organic products are USDA-certified. Every product listing clearly states its growing practices so you can make informed choices.

Our weekly subscription box comes in three sizes. You can customize items, skip weeks, or cancel anytime. Subscribers save 10% on every order and get priority delivery slots.
